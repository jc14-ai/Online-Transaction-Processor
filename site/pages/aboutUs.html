Kofei began when Paolo R. Reyes decided to take a step into the world of business by purchasing a branch of an established donut and coffee brand. While the donuts are not handmade, Paolo saw it as the perfect opportunity to explore passive income and learn how to run a business from the ground up. What started as a business move soon became a passion—serving quality products and sharing smiles, one cup and bite at a time.
We’re all about donuts and drinks simple, satisfying, and made with care. Our donuts are available by the piece or in bundles, perfect for treating yourself or sharing with others.
